Our story

The neighbourhood repair shop Toronto counts on.

N Appliance Repair opened its doors in 2017 because we believed Toronto deserved a different kind of appliance service. Not a faceless call centre. Not a rotating cast of contractors you never see twice. A genuine neighbourhood shop where real technicians answer the phone, remember your name, and stand behind every repair they do.

Our founder Nick spent years working for one of the big appliance chains before going independent. The pattern he saw over and over was the same: homeowners waiting half a day for a technician who showed up without the right parts, quoted a surprise price at the door, and disappeared after collecting the cheque. He started N Appliance Repair to prove it could be done better. One van, one phone number, one promise — show up on time, diagnose honestly, and fix it right the first visit.

That straightforward approach resonated across Toronto's neighbourhoods. Early customers in North York and Scarborough told their friends. Friends told their neighbours. Within a year the business had spread through Etobicoke, Mississauga, and Brampton through word of mouth alone, without a single paid advertisement. Today we serve the entire GTA with a small team of certified technicians who treat every home like it belongs to someone they know personally.

We remain a local, independent business on purpose. We do not franchise. We do not outsource calls to a dispatch centre in another province. When you phone (437) 524-1053, you reach someone who works in your community and can have a technician at your door the same day. Every repair comes with a 90-day parts and labour warranty because we want you to call us again, not because something broke, but because you trust us enough to recommend us to your family.

Over the years, we have repaired thousands of fridges, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, and stoves for families across Toronto. We work on every major brand — Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, GE, Frigidaire, Kenmore, and more. Our vans carry the most common replacement parts so most jobs are finished in a single visit, usually within the hour. We are proud that our 4.9-star rating reflects not just technical skill but the kind of personal, respectful service you expect from a small local business that depends on its reputation.

Certifications & training

Trained for every brand we touch.

Our technicians maintain active certifications and attend manufacturer training programs each year to stay current with new appliance technology.

Factory Brand Certifications
All technicians hold factory-issued service certifications from the brands they repair, including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, GE, and Frigidaire. These certifications require hands-on training and regular renewal.
Appliance Service Technology Diploma
Our technicians hold diplomas in Appliance Service Technology from accredited Ontario colleges. This formal training covers refrigeration systems, electrical diagnostics, gas appliances, and safety protocols.
Gas Appliance Safety (G3 Licence)
Technicians working on gas ranges, stoves, and cooktops hold Ontario G3 gas technician licences. This certification is legally required for gas appliance work in Ontario and ensures all gas repairs meet provincial safety codes.
Liability Insurance & Background Checks
Every team member carries full commercial liability insurance and has completed a criminal background check before being allowed to enter customer homes. This is mandatory, not optional, for everyone on our team.
Smart Appliance & Inverter Technology
Modern appliances are increasingly software-driven. Our technicians complete ongoing training in smart appliance diagnostics, inverter drive systems, and Wi-Fi connected appliance servicing for all major brands.
Annual Refresher Training
Every year each technician completes a minimum of 40 hours of refresher and update training. This covers new model releases, revised repair procedures, and updated safety requirements from manufacturers and regulators.
