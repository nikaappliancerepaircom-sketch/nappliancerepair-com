About N Appliance Repair

A family-owned appliance repair company serving Toronto and the GTA since 2015. We built our reputation one honest repair at a time.

Our story

A Toronto repair business built on trust.

N Appliance Repair started in 2015 as a one-van operation in North York. Founder Nick had spent years working for a large appliance chain and kept hearing the same frustration from customers: repair services were expensive, unreliable, and left people waiting all day with no call-ahead.

He launched N Appliance Repair with a simple commitment: show up on time, diagnose honestly, quote upfront, and fix it right the first time. Word spread through neighbourhoods in North York and Scarborough, and within two years the business had expanded to cover all of Toronto.

Today N Appliance Repair serves the entire GTA from Toronto to Oakville to Oshawa. We have a team of factory-trained technicians, a fleet of fully-stocked service vans, and over 5,200 five-star reviews from families across the region.

We are still a family business. Every customer is treated as a neighbour, every repair is backed by our 90-day warranty, and every technician who arrives at your door has been background-checked and fully trained.

Certifications & training

Trained for every brand we touch.

Our technicians maintain active certifications and attend manufacturer training programs each year to stay current with new appliance technology.

Factory Brand Certifications
All technicians hold factory-issued service certifications from the brands they repair, including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, GE, and Frigidaire. These certifications require hands-on training and regular renewal.
Appliance Service Technology Diploma
Our technicians hold diplomas in Appliance Service Technology from accredited Ontario colleges. This formal training covers refrigeration systems, electrical diagnostics, gas appliances, and safety protocols.
Gas Appliance Safety (G3 Licence)
Technicians working on gas ranges, stoves, and cooktops hold Ontario G3 gas technician licences. This certification is legally required for gas appliance work in Ontario and ensures all gas repairs meet provincial safety codes.
Liability Insurance & Background Checks
Every team member carries full commercial liability insurance and has completed a criminal background check before being allowed to enter customer homes. This is mandatory, not optional, for everyone on our team.
Smart Appliance & Inverter Technology
Modern appliances are increasingly software-driven. Our technicians complete ongoing training in smart appliance diagnostics, inverter drive systems, and Wi-Fi connected appliance servicing for all major brands.
Annual Refresher Training
Every year each technician completes a minimum of 40 hours of refresher and update training. This covers new model releases, revised repair procedures, and updated safety requirements from manufacturers and regulators.
