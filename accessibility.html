Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 30, 2026

N Appliance Repair is committed to providing accessible service consistent with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA).

Website accessibility

We aim for WCAG 2.1 Level AA on nappliancerepair.com. Specifically:

  • All informative images have descriptive alt text.
  • Pages can be navigated by keyboard alone (Tab/Shift-Tab/Enter).
  • Forms include explicit <label> elements for screen readers.
  • Colour contrast meets AA thresholds for body text.
  • You can resize text up to 200% without loss of functionality.

Service accessibility

  • Phone bookings — if the website is hard to use, call (437) 524-1053 and we'll book you over the phone.
  • Service animals and support persons are welcome at every appointment.
  • Alternative formats — quotes and invoices can be sent in plain-text email or large print on request.

Feedback

Encountered an accessibility barrier? We want to know. Email info@nappliancerepair.com with the subject "Accessibility feedback" and we'll respond within 5 business days.
