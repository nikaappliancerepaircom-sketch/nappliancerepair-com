What Are the Most Common LG Fridge Problems?

LG manufactures high-feature refrigerators with advanced electronics, which means there are more components that can fail compared to basic models. Our Toronto technicians have identified the failures that appear most consistently across LG fridge lines:

Defrost System Failure

LG French Door and bottom-freezer models are prone to defrost heater and defrost sensor failures. The pattern is familiar: the fridge cools well for 3–4 weeks, then gradually warms over 1–2 weeks as ice builds up on the evaporator coils. Manual defrost temporarily resolves it, but the problem returns. Our senior technician Maria S. handles this repair regularly across North York and identifies it as the most common LG service call she receives.

Ice Maker Fan Motor Failure (Er IF Error Code)

LG French Door models with in-door ice makers frequently develop problems with the ice maker compartment fan motor. The error code Er IF (or Er 1F) appears on the display, and ice production stops. The fan motor assembly in the ice maker compartment needs replacement. This is a straightforward repair that restores full ice production.

Linear Compressor Failure

LG French Door and side-by-side models from approximately 2014–2020 used a linear compressor design that has a higher-than-expected failure rate. When the compressor fails, the fridge makes a clicking noise as it tries to start, then stops cooling entirely. LG extended their compressor warranty to 10 years on these models — check whether your unit is still covered. If not, compressor replacement is expensive ($400–$700 CAD) and the repair-vs-replace calculation is important to work through carefully.

Ice Maker Assembly Failures

Beyond the fan motor, LG ice makers have a high rate of overall assembly failures — water inlet valve leaks, ice maker module failures, and auger motor issues. The ice maker for LG French Door models is a self-contained assembly that is typically replaced as a unit rather than repaired component by component.

Control Board Issues

LG main control boards and display boards can fail, causing erratic temperature readings, non-responsive controls, or random error codes. Control board replacement is more expensive than mechanical component repairs but restores the full smart functionality of the refrigerator.

LG Common Error Codes

  • Er IF / Er 1F: Ice maker fan motor failure
  • Er FF: Freezer fan motor failure
  • Er CF: Condenser fan motor failure
  • Er rF: Refrigerator fan motor failure
  • Er dH: Defrost heater or sensor issue
  • Er FS: Freezer sensor failure

How to Check Before Calling

1

Read the error code

LG fridges display error codes that tell you exactly what has failed. Note the code and relay it to your technician — this allows us to bring the correct replacement part on the first visit.

2

Check the LG compressor warranty

LG extended the warranty on linear compressors to 10 years for affected models. Visit LG Canada's website or call LG customer service with your model number to check coverage before paying for compressor repair.

3

Note the cooling pattern

Gradual cooling loss over weeks = defrost system. Sudden complete failure with clicking = likely compressor. Only the fridge warm but freezer fine = fan motor or damper. Tell your technician which pattern you observed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most common LG refrigerator problems?
The most common LG fridge problems are linear compressor failures (in LG French Door and side-by-side models), ice maker malfunctions, and cooling issues caused by defrost system failures. LG linear compressors from 2014–2019 have a known failure rate and were subject to a class action settlement in the US.
What does the LG fridge error code Er IF mean?
Error code Er IF (or Er 1F) on an LG refrigerator indicates a problem with the ice maker fan motor in the freezer. The ice maker compartment fan has stopped or is running slowly. This requires replacing the ice maker fan motor assembly.
How much does LG fridge repair cost in Toronto?
Most LG fridge repairs in Toronto run $150–$350 CAD including parts and labour. Defrost system repairs are $150–$230 CAD. Ice maker fan motor replacement is $170–$250 CAD. Linear compressor replacement is $400–$700 CAD — on older units, this cost may justify replacement.
Is it worth repairing an LG French Door fridge?
LG French Door refrigerators are high-value appliances ($1,500–$3,000+ CAD new). For defrost issues, fan failures, and ice maker problems, repair at $150–$350 CAD is almost always worthwhile on units under 10 years old. Linear compressor failures are the exception — get a quote and compare against replacement cost.
