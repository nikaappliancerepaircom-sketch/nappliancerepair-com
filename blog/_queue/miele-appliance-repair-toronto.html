Miele Dishwasher Repair Toronto

Miele dishwashers are among the most reliable on the market, but when they do develop faults, the issues tend to be specific to Miele engineering:

  • F11 / F14 errors — drain pump or drain hose issues. The Miele drain pump can be blocked by food debris or fail after years of use.
  • F12 / F13 errors — water intake problems. Usually a blocked inlet filter or a faulty aquastop valve.
  • F24 / F25 errors — heating element or temperature sensor failure. The Miele flow-through heater is a common wear item in older models.
  • Dishwasher not cleaning properly — blocked spray arms, worn pump, or incorrect detergent dosing.

Miele tip: Run a Miele machine cleaner cycle every 3 months and use Miele-branded descaler in Toronto where water hardness is moderate. This prevents lime buildup on the heating element and pump seals.

Miele Washing Machine Repair Toronto

Miele front-load washing machines are built to last, but common faults we see in Toronto include:

  • Door lock failure — Miele door interlock failures are more common in machines over 8 years old. The machine will not start and displays an F63 or similar door lock error.
  • Drum bearing wear — Miele bearings are high-quality, but after years of use they can develop noise or vibration. Drum bearing replacement extends machine life by another 5 or more years.
  • Programme selector faults — the rotary programme selector on classic Miele washers can develop intermittent contact issues over time.
  • Water not heating — the heating element or NTC temperature sensor has failed.

Miele Dryer Repair Toronto

Miele condenser and heat pump dryers are popular in Toronto condo buildings due to their ventless operation. Common issues include:

  • Not drying properly — the condenser unit needs cleaning every 20-30 cycles. Pull out the lower front panel and rinse the condenser under cold water. This is a maintenance task, not a repair.
  • F11 / F18 error codes — condenser fan motor or main motor faults. The fan circulates hot air through the drum.
  • Heat pump dryer faults — Miele heat pump dryers have a sealed refrigerant circuit that should only be serviced by a qualified technician with refrigerant handling certification.

Important for condo residents: Miele heat pump dryers should NOT be modified to use external venting. The sealed system is designed to work ventless. Running improper modifications voids the warranty and can cause damage.
