Why Do Maytag Dishwashers Fail?

Maytag dishwashers are built tough, but Toronto's hard water is particularly hard on the components that move water. Calcium and mineral deposits gradually restrict spray arm jets, clog the filter basket, and coat the heating element — reducing cleaning performance over months rather than all at once. Many customers notice their dishes are getting progressively less clean before a full breakdown occurs.

Beyond hard water damage, the most common Maytag mechanical failures we see at N Appliance Repair involve the drain pump and the door latch assembly. The drain pump motor on older Maytag models (particularly the MDB series) has a known tendency to seize after 7–10 years of use. The door latch spring can weaken or break, preventing the cycle from starting altogether.

What Are the Top Maytag Dishwasher Problems in Toronto?

  • Not draining — standing water after cycle — Drain pump failure or blocked drain filter/hose are the top causes.
  • Not cleaning dishes — food residue remaining — Clogged spray arms, a failing wash pump, or a gunked-up filter assembly.
  • Making grinding or humming noise — A foreign object in the chopper or a failing pump motor bearing.
  • Not starting — door latch issue — The door latch switch tells the control board the door is closed. If it fails, the cycle will not begin.
  • Leaking from door — A worn or cracked door gasket is the usual culprit.
  • Not filling with water — A faulty water inlet valve or a float switch stuck in the closed position.

How to Diagnose Your Maytag Dishwasher Before Calling

1

Check and clean the drain filter

Twist out the cylindrical filter at the bottom of the tub. Rinse it under hot water with a soft brush. A clogged filter is the most overlooked cause of poor cleaning and incomplete draining.

2

Inspect and clear the spray arms

Remove the lower and upper spray arms by turning the centre cap counterclockwise. Use a toothpick to clear any blocked holes, then rinse thoroughly before reinstalling.

3

Test the door latch

Close the door and listen for a firm click. If the door feels loose or the dishwasher does not respond when the start button is pressed, the latch switch may need replacement.

4

Check the drain hose for kinks

Pull the dishwasher out slightly (after disconnecting power) and inspect the drain hose behind and below the unit. A kinked hose will prevent proper drainage regardless of pump condition.

Warning: Before pulling out your Maytag dishwasher to inspect the drain hose or inlet valve, always disconnect power at the circuit breaker and close the water supply valve under the sink. Working on a powered, water-connected appliance can result in electrical shock or flooding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my Maytag dishwasher not cleaning dishes properly?
Poor cleaning in a Maytag dishwasher is typically caused by clogged spray arm holes, a worn or failed wash pump, insufficient water temperature, or hard water scale buildup on the spray arms and filter. Cleaning the filter and spray arms is a good first step.
How much does Maytag dishwasher repair cost in Toronto?
Maytag dishwasher repair in Toronto typically costs $160–$300 CAD including parts and labour. Simple fixes like a door latch or drain filter are at the lower end, while a wash motor or control board replacement is at the higher end.
Why is my Maytag dishwasher making a loud grinding noise?
A grinding noise during the wash cycle usually means a foreign object (glass shard, bone fragment) has entered the pump or chopper assembly. A humming or buzzing noise that stops suddenly often points to a seized drain pump motor.
