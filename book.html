Booking FAQ

How does online booking work? +
Select your preferred date and 2-hour arrival window from the booking calendar above. You receive instant confirmation. Our technician calls 30 minutes before arrival. The $89 diagnostic fee is waived when you approve the repair — so you only pay for the actual work done.
Is same-day service available when booking online? +
Yes, same-day slots appear in real time if they are available for your area. For the fastest same-day booking, you can also call (437) 524-1053 directly — we serve Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Scarborough, North York, and all GTA cities.
What appliances can I book a repair for? +
You can book a repair for any household appliance: refrigerators, washing machines, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, ranges, and stoves. All major brands are supported — Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, GE, Frigidaire, Kenmore, KitchenAid, Miele, and more.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule? +
Call us at (437) 524-1053 and we will reschedule at no penalty. We understand that schedules change — we only ask for as much notice as possible so we can fill the slot for another customer.
