FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a same-day appointment? +
For same-day service, call (437) 524-1053 before noon Monday through Saturday. We confirm your appointment within 1 hour and provide a 2-hour arrival window. Our technicians cover the full GTA so response times are typically 1–3 hours.
What are your service hours? +
We operate Monday through Friday 8am–8pm, Saturday 8am–8pm, and Sunday 9am–6pm. We answer calls 7 days a week across Toronto and the GTA.
Can I book an appointment online instead of calling? +
Yes. Our online booking system at hub.fixlify.app shows real-time availability and provides instant confirmation — available 24/7. You can also call (437) 524-1053 to speak with our team directly.
Do you charge for the diagnostic visit? +
Our diagnostic fee is $80 and covers the technician's visit, inspection, and written quote. This fee is waived entirely when you proceed with the repair — so if you approve the repair, the diagnostic is free. Our repair costs typically range from $150 to $450 depending on the appliance and fault type.
