Common Dryer Failures in Willowdale

Heating element failure is the most common dryer repair we perform in Willowdale. Electric dryers use a coiled wire element that glows red-hot during operation, and over time the wire fatigues and breaks, leaving the dryer tumbling but producing no heat. Clothes tumble for a full cycle and come out damp. In gas dryers — still common in Willowdale's older homes — the equivalent failure is a worn igniter that can no longer reach the temperature needed to open the gas valve. Dryer Maintenance Tips for Willowdale Residents

Preventive maintenance extends the life of your dryer and reduces the likelihood of emergency repair calls. Here are the most important maintenance practices for Willowdale homeowners:

  • Clean the lint trap before every single load — a clogged lint screen reduces efficiency by 30% and is a fire hazard
  • Have the dryer vent duct professionally cleaned once a year — lint buildup in the vent is the leading cause of dryer fires in Canada
  • Check the exterior vent flap to make sure it opens when the dryer runs and closes when it stops — a stuck flap lets cold air and pests into the vent line
  • Don't overload the dryer — clothes need room to tumble for even drying, and overloading strains the belt and motor
  • Wipe down the moisture sensor bars inside the drum monthly with rubbing alcohol — fabric softener residue coats these sensors and causes inaccurate cycle times

Following these maintenance steps can add years to your dryer's lifespan and help you avoid the most common repair issues we see in Willowdale. If you're unsure about any maintenance procedure, call us at (437) 524-1053 — we're happy to walk you through it over the phone.

Warning Signs Your Dryer Needs Repair

These warning signs mean your dryer needs professional attention:

  • Clothes taking two or more cycles to dry completely
  • Dryer exterior or laundry room feels unusually hot during operation
  • Burning smell coming from the dryer during use
  • Loud thumping, squealing, or grinding sounds when the drum turns
  • Dryer starts but stops after a few minutes without completing the cycle
  • Lint accumulating on clothes even after cleaning the lint trap
