Refrigerator Maintenance for East York Bungalows

Simple maintenance steps prevent the most common fridge failures in East York homes. The compact kitchen layouts mean condenser coils — located at the back of most older fridges — collect dust, pet hair, and cooking grease faster than in more ventilated spaces. Cleaning the coils twice a year with a vacuum and coil brush takes 15 minutes and can prevent compressor overheating and early failure.

  • Vacuum condenser coils at the back or bottom of the fridge every 6 months
  • Check door gaskets for cracks — especially bottom corners where condensation accumulates
  • Keep the fridge at 3°C and freezer at -18°C — verify with an appliance thermometer
  • Replace the water filter annually if your model has a water dispenser
  • Leave 2–3 inches of clearance at the back for air circulation, even in tight kitchen alcoves
  • Don't block the interior vents between fridge and freezer compartments with food items
