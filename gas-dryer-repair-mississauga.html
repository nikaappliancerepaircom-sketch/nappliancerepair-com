Gas Dryer Safety Procedures

A gas dryer in your Mississauga laundry area burns natural gas to generate heat. Mississauga Fire and Emergency Services recommends CO detectors on every floor of homes with gas appliances. Recognizing warning signs and responding correctly can prevent serious harm.

  1. If you smell gas near your dryer in Mississauga, shut off the gas supply valve behind the unit if you can reach it safely
  2. Open the nearest window or exterior door to ventilate the laundry area
  3. Do not start or stop the dryer — the electrical switch can produce a spark
