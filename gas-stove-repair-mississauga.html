Gas Stove Emergency Response Steps

Gas stoves produce open flames in your Mississauga kitchen. Enbridge supplies natural gas throughout Mississauga via the Peel Region distribution network. When a gas stove malfunctions, the response protocol differs from electric appliance failures.

  1. Turn all burner knobs on your Mississauga gas stove to the OFF position immediately
  2. Do not attempt to relight a burner that has gone out — unburned gas may have pooled above the cooktop
  3. Open kitchen windows and the nearest exterior door for cross-ventilation
  4. If gas odour persists with all controls off, evacuate and dial 911 from outside
