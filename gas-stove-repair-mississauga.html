  1. Turn all burner knobs on your Mississauga gas stove to the OFF position immediately
  2. Do not attempt to relight a burner that has gone out — unburned gas may have pooled above the cooktop
  3. Open kitchen windows and the nearest exterior door for cross-ventilation

My Mississauga gas stove oven is not reaching the set temperature — why?+

The oven igniter glow bar is the most likely culprit. As the silicon carbide element ages, it draws progressively less electrical current. When amperage drops below about 3.0 amps, the gas safety valve cannot open fully, and the oven undershoots the target temperature by 25 to 50 degrees.
