Questions

Frequently asked.

What brands do you repair? +
We repair all major appliance brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Bosch, Frigidaire, Kenmore, Maytag, KitchenAid, Electrolux, Miele, and Amana. If your brand is not listed, call us at (437) 524-1053 — we service virtually every residential appliance brand sold in Canada.
How much does appliance repair cost in Toronto? +
Diagnostic visits are $80, which is fully waived if you proceed with the repair. Standard repairs typically run $150–$280 including parts and labour. Complex repairs involving compressors or control boards range from $280–$450. You receive a firm written quote before any work begins — the price never changes.
Do you offer same-day service? +
Yes. Same-day service is available Monday through Saturday 8am–8pm and Sunday 9am–6pm across Toronto and the GTA. Availability depends on your location and the time you call. We recommend calling before noon for the highest chance of same-day service.
What is your warranty? +
Every repair is covered by a 90-day warranty on both parts and labour. If the same issue recurs within 90 days of your repair, we will return and fix it at no charge. This warranty applies to all appliances and all brands we service.
What areas do you service? +
We serve all of Toronto including Scarborough, North York, and Etobicoke, as well as Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Markham, Oakville, Burlington, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, and Oshawa. If you are unsure whether we cover your area, call (437) 524-1053 and we will confirm immediately.
How long does a repair take? +
95% of repairs are completed in a single visit, typically within 1–2 hours. Our technicians carry OEM parts for all major brands in their service vans. In rare cases where a specific part must be ordered, we schedule a priority return visit as quickly as possible.
Do you repair both old and new appliances? +
Yes. We repair appliances of all ages and models. For very old appliances, we will give you an honest assessment. A general rule: if the repair costs less than 50% of a new appliance's value and the unit is under 10–12 years old, repair is almost always the better financial decision.
Are your technicians licensed? +
Yes. All N Appliance Repair technicians are factory-trained for every major brand they service, background-checked, and carry full liability insurance. We are a licensed and insured appliance repair company with 6+ years serving the GTA.
Do you offer free estimates? +
We charge an $80 diagnostic fee for the on-site visit, which is fully waived when you proceed with the repair. You receive a detailed written quote with no obligation before any work begins. The price we quote is the price you pay — no hidden fees.
