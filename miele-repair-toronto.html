Miele Toronto FAQ

Do you service Miele appliances with the specialist training required?
Yes. Miele appliances require specific diagnostic tools and training that most general repair companies lack. Our technicians have Miele-compatible equipment and experience with the full Miele lineup from dishwashers to washing machines to refrigerators.
Miele parts are expensive. Is it worth repairing?
Almost always yes. A Miele dishwasher costs $1,500 to $3,000 new. A $300 to $500 repair extends its life 5 to 10 years. Miele machines are designed to last 20 years, so early replacement is almost never the economical choice.
My Miele washing machine shows F53. What does it mean?
F53 is a motor communication fault, usually caused by a motor capacitor or control module issue. This is not a catastrophic failure — the machine is protecting itself. We diagnose the exact component and repair or replace it.
How much does Miele appliance repair cost?
Miele repairs are more expensive than mass-market brands due to parts cost and specialist labour. Typical repairs range from $200 to $600. However, given Miele's replacement cost ($1,500 to $4,000+) this is almost always the right financial decision.
