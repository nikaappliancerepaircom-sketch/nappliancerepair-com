Miele Washer F53 / F51 Error

F53 and F51 errors on Miele washers indicate motor communication faults or motor capacitor failures. Miele's brushless motors are highly durable but require specialist diagnosis. We use Miele-compatible diagnostic tools for accurate fault identification.

Miele Dishwasher Water Intake Fault

Miele dishwashers display "Water Intake/Drainage" errors from a faulty inlet valve, blocked water softener, or kinked supply hose. The Miele water softener requires regular salt replenishment and occasional service.

Miele Oven Door Hinge Failure

Miele oven door hinges are a known wear item after years of use. A drooping or misaligned door prevents proper sealing and temperature retention. We replace Miele hinge assemblies with genuine parts.
