Gas range repair Aurora

Aurora has a high proportion of natural gas connections in detached homes — most pre-2010 Aurora singles use gas ranges. Common gas range issues in Aurora: burner igniters crack after 5–8 years (the igniter glows but doesn't ignite the burner), gas valve solenoid coil failure, and oven bake burner flame sensor issues. Our gas-certified technicians handle all Aurora gas range repairs to TSSA standards. We carry igniters for Whirlpool/Maytag, GE, Samsung, and Wolf ranges in our York Region vans.

Electric and induction range repair Aurora

Electric oven repairs in Aurora most commonly involve bake element failure (the circular coil at the bottom of the oven cavity that burns out, often visibly), oven temperature sensor failure (oven overheats or never reaches temperature), or control board/clock failure. Induction cooktop repairs are growing in Aurora's newer builds — Samsung and Bosch induction element and control board issues are the most common.
