Gas stove repair Thornhill

Gas range issues are common in Thornhill's pre-2000 detached homes. We see high volumes of gas igniter failures — igniters typically last 5–10 years before cracking. An igniter that glows orange but doesn't ignite the burner (takes 10+ seconds) is failing and needs replacement.
