Service description

We provide in-home appliance diagnosis and repair across the Greater Toronto Area. We do not sell new appliances. We do not perform warranty work for manufacturers unless explicitly authorized in writing.

Quotes and pricing

  • A diagnostic visit is required before any repair work. The diagnostic fee is disclosed when you book and is waived when you proceed with the repair we quote.
  • All repair work is preceded by a written quote that includes parts and labour. We do not begin work until the quote is approved.
  • Final pricing matches the approved quote unless additional issues are uncovered during the repair, in which case we stop, contact you, and seek approval before continuing.

Warranty

Parts we install and labour we perform are covered for 90 days from the repair date. Warranty does not cover misuse, secondary failures unrelated to the original repair, or pre-existing defects we did not work on.

You can reschedule or cancel a booked repair free of charge by phone or email up to 2 hours before the appointment. Late cancellations may incur a trip fee equal to the diagnostic charge.
